How It Works:

  1. When you open ar_project.html or loader.html, it loads A-Frame and AR.js libraries

  3. When it detects the marker, a 3D flower appears
  4. The breathing.js script makes the flower scale up and down (inhale/exhale cycles)
  5. Each cycle is 10 seconds total (5s inhale + 5s exhale)
